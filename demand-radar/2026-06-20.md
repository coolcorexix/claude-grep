For what it's worth, our Ctrl-B branching writes a new JSONL with a fresh `sessionId` at the fork point, marks the original unchanged, and both branches appear in `claude --resume`. Also `/btw`-compatible. Happy to share the exact file layout if it's useful reference for what the correct behavior should look like here.
